Casual Cafe

Casual Café, established in 2018, is a family-owned, small town restaurant that specializes in homecooked meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The goal at Casual Café is for each of our guests to feel at home through the food and hospitality that we serve up. Casual’s owners, Shayne and Mike, take pride in the menu, as all of the recipes originated in their home and have been served for their families for decades. The menu spotlights fresh produce, locally harvested wild game, and signature down-home flavors. If you are in the area and on the hunt for good food, great people, and a one-of-a-kind atmosphere, swing by Casual Café!
