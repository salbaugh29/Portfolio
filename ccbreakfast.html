Breakfast Menu

Classic Breakfast

2 eggs (any style), your choice of meat (bacon, sausage, or ham), hashbrowns, and your choice of toast, English muffin, or a biscuit.

Eggs Benedict

Poached eggs and Canadian bacon served on a toasted English muffin, topped with rich Hollandaise sauce. Served with hashbrowns on the side.

Pancakes or French Toast

Your choice of 3 fluffy buttermilk pancakes or 2 slices of French toast. Served with 2 eggs (any style), and fresh fruit.

Breakfast Sandwich Meal

Breakfast sandwich created on your choice of bread, English muffin, or biscuit. To include bacon, sausage, or ham and your choice of cheese as well. Served with a crispy hashbrown patty.

Biscuits & Gravy

Two fresh baked buttermilk biscuits smothered in country sausage gravy. Served with hash browns.

Corned Beef Hash

Traditional corned beef hash, 2 eggs (any style). Served with your choice of toast, English muffin, or biscuit.
