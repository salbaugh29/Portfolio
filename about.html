About Casual Cafe

Casual Cafe is located in Shelton, the heart of Mason County. Whether you are locals craving some comfort food and some time away from your own kitchens, or out of towners looking for some great food and a warm welcome, we would love for you to drop in for a meal. The doors are open 7 days a week.
